About Us

Palace Royal International School is a Christian-based international institution committed to delivering first-class, value-driven education. The school focuses on holistic child development — academic, spiritual, emotional, and social — preparing students to excel locally and globally.

Mission

To impart knowledge, provide outstanding care, excellent education, and guide children in developing their gifts and abilities with honour.

Vision

We are a Christian school committed to delivering first-class, value-driven education focused on holistic development and global readiness.

Our Curriculum

Ghana Education Service (GES) Curriculum

Our learners follow the Ghana Education Service curriculum which provides a strong academic foundation in literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies. It ensures alignment with national educational standards and assessments.

Oxford International Curriculum

We offer a premium, future-focused education led by the Oxford International Curriculum (OIC), equipping students with global competencies, critical thinking skills, and a strong academic foundation in Mathematics, Science, English Language, Computing, Sustainability, Global Skills, and Wellbeing.

This is seamlessly complemented by the Ghana Education Service (GES) curriculum—covering History, Religious and Moral Education, Creative Arts, French, and Ghanaian Language (Asante Twi) to ensure cultural identity, national relevance, and academic balance.

Through enriched co-curricular learning with the Mezzo Maths Programme and diverse extra-curricular activities such as Ballet, Taekwondo, Piano and Violin lessons, Arts and Crafts, and Fashion Design, we nurture well-rounded, confident learners prepared to thrive both locally and globally.

Why Our Dual Curriculum Works

By combining Ghana Education Service curriculum and Oxford International Curriculum learning approaches, students benefit from both academic discipline and global readiness, giving them a competitive advantage in higher education and future careers.

Message from CEO

At Palace Royal International School, we believe that education goes beyond academic achievement. Our mission is to nurture well-rounded individuals who are grounded in faith, equipped with knowledge, and inspired to pursue excellence in all aspects of life.

We are committed to building not only brilliant minds but also strong character and moral integrity. As a Christian-based international school, we provide an environment where children are nurtured in Christ, guided by values, and empowered to discover and develop their unique gifts and potential.

Our policies, procedures, and operational frameworks are carefully designed to ensure professionalism, clarity, and consistency across all areas of the school. They reflect our dedication to high educational standards, effective leadership, and a culture of accountability and excellence.

By aligning our practices with national requirements and international best practices, we ensure that every child receives a globally relevant and future-ready education.

At Palace Royal International School, we foster a culture of collaboration among staff, parents, and partners. We believe education is a shared responsibility, and through unity of purpose, we create a nurturing environment where every child can thrive academically, socially, emotionally, and spiritually.

As a leadership team, we remain committed to continuous improvement, innovation, and service. Our ultimate goal is to raise confident, disciplined, and globally minded students who will make a positive impact in their communities and the world.

Rev. Dr Bernard Mensa Adams
